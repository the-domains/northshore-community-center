Welcome to the Lucerne Alpine Senior Center

Lucerne Alpine Senior Center

Originally the 'Lucerne Elementary School', this building has become a staple in the community for accessing local information and resources, serving as a hub for special events and social gatherings, and working hand-in-hand with local organizations and individuals to enhance and offer more to the community.

Making A Difference

Programs That Really Help

Meals on Wheels, only a part of the 'Elder Nutrition Program' offered at the center, delivers home bound meals to local seniors throughout Lucerne, Nice, Upper Lake, and Blue Lakes. The Northshore Community Center is dedicated to serving the populations of this area, with a special focus on enhancing the lives of elders.
