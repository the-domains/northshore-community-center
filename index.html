Welcome to the Northshore Community Center

Northshore Community Center

Originally the 'Lucerne Elementary School', this building has become a staple in the community for accessing local information and resources, serving as a hub for special events and social gatherings, and working hand-in-hand with local organizations and individuals to enhance the North shore.

Making A Difference

Programs That Really Help

Meals on Wheels, only a part of the 'Elder Nutrition Program' offered at the center, delivers home bound meals to local seniors throughout Lucerne, Nice, Upper Lake, and Blue Lakes. The Northshore Community Center is dedicated to serving the populations of this area, with a special focus on enhancing the lives of elders.

Make It Your Own

An Open Space for Community Organizing

For visionary people wishing to create something new and exciting, perhaps by arranging a group or meeting, holding classes, organizing social events and gatherings, hosting concerts and entertainment, serving food and drinks, you name it... The Northshore Community Center is a prime venue and can help make it happen.

And You Can Too...

From the volunteers at the NCC Thrift Store, to the volunteers in the NCC Food Pantry, all the way up to the volunteers serving on the Board of Directors of LASC, this organization is carried by the efforts of the generous and hardworking people of the North shore and beyond, and their contributions. For those who contribute their time, effort, and resources so generously to this community, we are privileged to say 'Thank You'.
